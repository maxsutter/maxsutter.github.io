What You Get Out of Working With Me

Clients come to me when they realize their own personality, beliefs, or emotions are blocking their success. Together, we clear what’s in the way, creating more ease, joy, and results.

The Game You’re Playing

We’re unhappy when we don’t get what we want or get what we don’t want. Many try to control the world to fit their desires—and if you’re reading this, you’ve likely succeeded to some extent. But this game never ends. Challenges persist, and the same issues often return.

There’s another way: the inner game. Instead of fighting the world, you learn to enjoy it as it is by understanding and shifting your "operating system"—your thoughts, emotions, and beliefs. Changing these makes you both happier and more effective.

Where I can help

Do you feel stuck or under constant pressure? Maybe you’re isolated in your role, dealing with tension with a co-founder, boss, or investor, or struggling to balance company growth with your personal needs. Perhaps a big decision—like quitting, pivoting, or restructuring—feels overwhelming.

Maybe you can’t be yourself at work, fearing the consequences. These challenges can leave even the strongest leaders feeling lost.

I help you tackle challenges like these by uncovering what you’re not yet seeing. Together, we’ll resolve what’s holding you back, so you can achieve better results with more joy and ease.

About Me

I’ve been where you are—founder, CEO, leader. I know the highs of building something remarkable and the lows of feeling stuck or pressured. I’ve updated my own outdated "operating system"—the beliefs and patterns holding me back—and transformed my leadership and life. Through this process, I discovered that guiding others through similar challenges is the most fulfilling part of leadership for me.

With 20+ years of meditation and emotional development, and training from top Silicon Valley coaches, I bring insights from Western psychology and Eastern wisdom. This lets me meet you wherever you are, whether stuck, unseen, or ready to grow.

Outside coaching, I’m with my wife and kids, embracing life’s simple joys.

My Approach

Surfacing the Iceberg

Problems are like icebergs—the visible part is small. Beneath lies unexamined beliefs and patterns from your past. Coaching helps bring this material into the light, enabling leadership with clarity, connection, and joy.

Updating Your Operating System

Beliefs and strategies formed early in life often outlive their usefulness. Together, we replace these with patterns that empower you.

A Deeper Exploration

Rather than surface-level fixes, we explore your unseen assumptions, patterns, and limiting beliefs. I hold a safe, agenda-free space for you to uncover your genius at your pace.

Integrating Eastern Insights and your Full Self

My coaching blends modern leadership practices with Buddhist wisdom. It’s about clearing blocks, addressing emotions, and creating space for joy, connection, and effectiveness.

At its core, coaching offers freedom—freedom from old fears and patterns, with joy and connection as the ultimate goals. Effectiveness and results will come by themselves.
