Quick Reality Check

Are you…

  • Successful on paper but quietly unhappy or burned out?
  • Avoiding conflicts with your co-founders, boss, or team until they blow up?
  • Micromanaging because you just can’t find “great people”?

My Approach

Under the Surface

We look at what’s really running the show—unconscious beliefs, emotional baggage, all that stuff quietly sabotaging you. We bring it to light and transform it.

Updating Your “Operating System”

Your coping strategies made sense in the past, but rn they’re blocking your flow. We peel away layers of fear, shame, and limiting beliefs so your natural clarity and leadership can shine.

Presence Over Tactics

Tactics are cool, but without inner alignment, they feel forced and don’t stick. My coaching is about deep change so better leadership, relationships, and decisions flow easily from who you are, not who you “should” be.

Blending East & West

I integrate mindfulness, emotional healing, and proven leadership insights. The result? A holistic approach that hits deeper than standard “business coaching.”
