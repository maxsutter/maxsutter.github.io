About Me

I’ve walked a similar path to the one you’re on—as a founder, CEO, and leader navigating the high-stakes terrain of fast-growing companies. I know the thrill of building something extraordinary, but I also know how isolating it can feel when the challenges stack up, pressure rises, and the answers aren’t clear.

What makes my coaching unique is that I’ve been there. I’ve faced those moments of self-doubt, frustration, and overwhelm, and I’ve worked through them with the help of incredible coaches. Along the way, I learned to update my own “operating system”—the old, outdated beliefs and patterns about success, connection, and leadership that were holding me back. That journey transformed my leadership, my life, and my relationships.

Today, I integrate that firsthand experience with insights from over 20 years of serious meditation practice and emotional development. I’ve learned coaching from some of the best coaches in Silicon Valley and draw from both Western psychology and Eastern wisdom. This depth of experience allows me to meet my clients where they are, whether they feel stuck, unseen, or ready for a new level of leadership.

When I’m not coaching, meditating, or building, I spend my time with my wife and our two young children, exploring the joys of life together.

My Approach

Every problem you bring is like the tip of an iceberg—what’s visible is only a fraction of the real story. Beneath the surface lie old beliefs, unexamined assumptions, and patterns from the past that continue to shape your leadership and your life. My coaching is about bringing that unconscious material into the light, giving you the freedom to lead with clarity, joy, and connection.

Think of it like updating an outdated operating system. Many of the beliefs and strategies you use—how you think the world works, what you think will bring you success or love—were installed early in life, often by parents, teachers, or past experiences. They worked once but no longer serve you, and now they hold you back. Together, we identify and replace those old patterns, so you can move forward with more power, joy, and effectiveness.

I don’t offer surface-level techniques or cookie-cutter solutions. Instead, we explore the unseen together: your assumptions, limiting beliefs, repeating patterns, and incomplete mental maps. I don’t have an agenda (unlike your boss, investor, or spouse). I’m your partner in exploration, holding a safe and loving space for you to uncover what’s truly going on, at your pace. This isn’t about making you fit someone else’s mold—it’s about helping you step into your own genius.

My coaching combines insights from modern leadership development and timeless Eastern practices, especially from the Buddhist path. It’s not just about your mind—it’s about your whole self. We’ll dissolve what’s holding you back, clear the emotions that keep you stuck, and unlock a deeper sense of joy, connection, and effectiveness.

At its core, my coaching is about freedom: freedom from the old patterns, beliefs, and fears that no longer serve you. And while joy and connection are the true goals, effectiveness will be a welcome side product.
