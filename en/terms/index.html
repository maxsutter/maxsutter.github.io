1. Scope of Application

These GTC apply to all contracts for Executive Coaching between Maximilian O. Sutter, Harbatshofen 10 ½, 88167 Stiefenhofen ("Coach") and his clients, unless otherwise agreed in writing. Clients may be companies or private individuals. These GTC apply exclusively to services in the field of Executive Coaching. Deviating terms and conditions shall not become part of the contract unless the Coach expressly agrees to their application. These GTC are deemed accepted if the client agrees to them prior to contract conclusion or books a coaching service with knowledge thereof. For clients who are consumers within the meaning of § 13 BGB, the mandatory statutory consumer protection regulations apply in addition.

2. Services of the Coach

The Coach offers personal coaching services for professional and personal development. Sessions are intended for reflection, orientation, and goal clarification. No specific outcome is guaranteed, nor is any success liability assumed.

Coaching is not a substitute for therapy or professional advice (e.g., psychological, medical, or legal consultation). Coaching requires normal mental resilience. Health-related limitations must be communicated to the Coach in advance.

Coaching sessions are personally conducted by the Coach. The Coach may record or transcribe sessions (e.g., via fireflies.ai or Google Workspace) to improve the quality of the work or to create summaries. These data may be analyzed using AI-supported systems (e.g., ChatGPT). Clients may object to such processing at any time; from that point on, no further processing will take place.

3. Coaching Models

3.1 Retainer Model

The Retainer Model includes 2–4 coaching sessions per month (average duration: 70–80 minutes), as well as individual preparation and follow-up, and asynchronous support via Signal or WhatsApp ("Fair Use"). Communication takes place on weekdays during regular business hours. Continuous availability is not owed.

Sessions must be booked independently by clients. Appointments canceled less than 24 hours before the scheduled time or missed entirely will be forfeited. (The Coach will strive to be accommodating.) If canceled at least 24 hours in advance, the appointment can be rescheduled free of charge within 4 weeks. Thereafter, the entitlement expires. Exceptions apply to cancellations or failures for which the Coach is responsible.

Payment is due monthly in advance. Invoices are issued on the 1st calendar day of each month and payable within 7 days.

The minimum term is 6 months. Thereafter, the contract may be terminated monthly. Termination is possible at any time within the first three sessions; any fees already paid will be fully refunded. Termination must be made in text form (e.g., via email).

Upon request, additional sessions outside the agreed retainer scope may be held. These will be separately agreed upon and billed.

3.2 Single Session Billing

Coachings can also be booked and billed individually. A session typically lasts 70 minutes (between 60 and 80 minutes). Travel time and costs are billed separately for on-site sessions (€300 flat rate within Germany plus any overnight costs and shared travel time from 1 hour onward). Special agreements are possible.

Cancellations up to 24 hours in advance are free of charge. For later cancellations or no-shows, the full fee is due unless the appointment is rescheduled by mutual agreement.

The Coach may cancel appointments for good cause (e.g., illness, technical issues); a replacement date will then be offered. No further claims exist.

4. Fees and Payment Terms

All prices are net unless otherwise stated. The fee is determined by individual agreement. Invoices are payable within 7 days without deduction. In the event of payment default, the statutory regulations (§ 286, § 288 BGB) apply, including default interest, a €40 flat fee (for non-consumers), and any reminder fees. The Coach may claim further damages.

5. Confidentiality and Data Protection

Decisions based on coaching are the sole responsibility of the clients. The Coach is not liable for delays or failures caused by force majeure or similar circumstances.

7. Term and Termination

Individual contracts and framework agreements may be terminated with two weeks' notice to the end of the month unless a different term has been agreed. Already agreed appointments will be billed.
